Exam 1

  1. Question

    Which of the following cities are the capital of the corresponding country?


    1. Lagos (Nigeria)
    2. Warsaw (Poland)
    3. Istanbul (Turkey)
    4. Astana (Kazakhstan)
    5. Tokyo (Japan)

    Solution

    1. False. The capital of Nigeria is Abuja.
    2. True. Warsaw is the capital of Poland.
    3. False. The capital of Turkey is Ankara.
    4. True. Astana is the capital of Kazakhstan.
    5. True. Tokyo is the capital of Japan.
